2. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases as permitted by Isle of Man Data Protection Act 2018:

  • Contract Performance: To fulfill orders and provide services you've requested
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with legal requirements (e.g., financial record-keeping)
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services and prevent fraud
  • Consent: For marketing communications (where required)

4.1 Order Processing

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmations and updates

  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized transactions

  • Resolve disputes

4.3 Marketing (With Your Consent)

Your name and delivery address are shared with courier services (e.g., Royal Mail, DHL) to deliver your orders.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

8. Your Rights

Under Isle of Man data protection law, you have the right to:

8.1 Access Your Data

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

8.2 Correct Your Data

Ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.

8.3 Delete Your Data

Request deletion of your data (subject to legal record-keeping requirements).

8.4 Object to Processing

Object to our use of your data for marketing or legitimate interests.

8.5 Data Portability

Receive your data in a machine-readable format to transfer to another service.

8.6 Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for marketing at any time (click "unsubscribe" in emails).

To Exercise Your Rights: Email andy@manxcrowndiamonds.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Security

9.1 Security Measures

  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you within 72 hours and report to the Isle of Man Information Commissioner if required by law.
