About Andy Asbridge

The story behind Manx Crown Diamonds

👤

Andy Asbridge

Owner & Director

Welcome to Manx Crown Diamonds. I'm Andy Asbridge, the Owner and Director of Manx Crown Diamonds, and I'm passionate about bringing a personal touch to the art of jewellery.

Based in the heart of the Isle of Man, I specialise in sourcing IGI and GIA Certified diamonds to create bespoke pieces that are as unique as you are. For me, it's not just about crafting jewellery – it's about building connections and creating heirlooms that will be treasured for generations.

I love working directly with my customers, combining timeless craftsmanship with the natural beauty of our island to bring your jewellery dreams to life. Whether you're looking for the perfect engagement ring, a custom design, or a meaningful gift, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.

Every piece that leaves my workshop carries with it a commitment to excellence, a dedication to quality, and a promise of enduring beauty. I believe that jewellery should tell a story – your story – and I'm honored to help you write it.
