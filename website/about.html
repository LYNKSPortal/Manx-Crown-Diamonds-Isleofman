About Us

Luxury jewellery — Bespoke pieces built around your vision

Manx Crown Diamonds

At Manx Crown Diamonds, we believe that exceptional jewellery should be more than beautiful — it should tell your story. Based on the Isle of Man, we exist to bring the highest standards of craftsmanship and personal service to every client, whether you come to us with a clear vision or simply the beginnings of an idea.

From our collection of handcrafted diamond rings, necklaces, bracelets and earrings to fully custom bespoke commissions, everything we do is guided by one principle: creating pieces that will be treasured for a lifetime.

What We Do

Three things at the heart of everything we create

Luxury Jewellery

Our ready-to-configure collection spans diamond rings, necklaces, bracelets, earrings and watches — each piece available in your choice of metal, stone size and finish. Every item is crafted to the same exacting standard, using only IGI and GIA certified diamonds.

Bespoke Commissions

Have something unique in mind? We specialise in bringing bespoke visions to life. Whether it’s a one-of-a-kind engagement ring, a meaningful gift or a family heirloom reimagined, we work with you from first sketch to finished piece to make it exactly right.

Expert Craftsmanship

Every piece we sell is a reflection of the care and pride we take in our work. Rooted in the Isle of Man, we combine timeless jewellery-making traditions with a deeply personal approach — treating every commission as if it were our own.

Bespoke Service

We Bring Your Vision to Life

No two bespoke commissions are alike, and that’s exactly the point. The bespoke process is a collaboration — you bring the ideas, the inspiration, the story behind the piece; we bring the expertise, the craftsmanship and the certified diamonds to make it real.

We take the time to understand what you want and why, then guide you through every decision — from the setting and stone shape to the metal and finishing details — so the end result is something that feels completely and unmistakably yours.
