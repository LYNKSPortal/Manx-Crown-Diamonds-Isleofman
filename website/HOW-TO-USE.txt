## 🔗 How Navigation Works:

- **Home** → Click collections → View category → Add to cart
- **Collections** → Browse all categories
- **Cart Icon** → View your shopping cart
- **Add to Cart** → Items save in your browser
- All links work between pages!

## 🛒 Shopping Cart:

- Click "Add to Cart" on any product
- Cart icon shows item count
- Click cart icon to view cart
- Increase/decrease quantities with +/- buttons
- Click "Proceed to Checkout" to complete order

## 💎 To Add Your Products:

Open any category page (like **rings.html**) in a text editor and find this section:

```javascript
const allProducts = [
    {
        id: 1, 
        name: "Your Product Name",
        category: "rings",
        price: 3500,
        description: "Your description",
        icon: "💍"  // Replace with your image path
    },
```

Change:
- **name** - Your product name
- **price** - Your price
- **description** - Your description
- **icon** - Replace "💍" with path to your photo like "images/ring1.jpg"

## 📸 To Add Photos:

1. Create an "images" folder
2. Put your product photos in it
3. Replace icon: "💍" with icon: "images/yourphoto.jpg"
