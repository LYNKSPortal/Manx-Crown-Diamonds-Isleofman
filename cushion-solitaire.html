Ring Size Guide

Not sure of your size? Use one of the methods below to find your perfect fit.

Method 1 — Measure an Existing Ring

Place the ring flat on a ruler and measure the inside diameter in mm, then match to the chart below.

Method 2 — Measure Your Finger

  1. Wrap a thin strip of paper around the base of your finger.
  2. Mark where it meets and measure the length in mm.
  3. Match to the table below.
💡 Tip: Fingers are larger in the evening — measure then for the most accurate fit.
