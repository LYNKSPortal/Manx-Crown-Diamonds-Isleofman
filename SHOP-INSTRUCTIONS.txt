🔧 CUSTOMIZATION:
-----------------

To add more products:
1. Open rings-shop.html in text editor
2. Copy a product card section
3. Change: name, price, description
4. Update the onclick function with new details

To change prices:
1. Edit the product cards in rings-shop.html
2. Edit the configurator prices in oval-solitaire-product-updated.html
3. Find: data-price="2500" and change amounts

To add product photos:
1. Convert your image to base64
2. Replace the emoji icons with: <img src="data:image/jpeg;base64,YOUR_BASE64">
