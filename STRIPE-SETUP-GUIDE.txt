HOW IT WORKS:
-------------
1. Customer fills out checkout form
2. Enters card details (secured by Stripe)
3. Clicks "Complete Purchase"
4. Payment is authorized (but not charged yet)
5. You receive email with:
   - Order details
   - Payment Method ID
   - Card last 4 digits
6. YOU manually charge in Stripe dashboard OR
7. Set up automatic charging (requires backend server)

FEES:
-----
UK Cards: 1.5% + 20p per transaction
EU Cards: 2.5% + 20p per transaction
International: 3.25% + 20p per transaction

Example: £5,000 sale = £5,000 - £75.20 (fee) = £4,924.80 in your account

WHEN YOU GET PAID:
------------------
- First payout: 7-14 days after first sale
- After that: Every 2-7 days automatically
- Money goes directly to your bank account

MANUAL CHARGING (Current Setup):
---------------------------------
1. Receive order email with Payment Method ID
2. Log into Stripe dashboard
3. Go to: Payments → Create Payment
4. Enter: Payment Method ID and amount
5. Click "Charge"
6. Money is processed!

AUTOMATIC CHARGING (Advanced):
